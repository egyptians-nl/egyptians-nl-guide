# Residence Permit

Residence permit rules depend heavily on your purpose of stay and personal situation. Use this page as orientation only, then verify everything with IND.

## IND is the main official source

The Immigration and Naturalisation Service (`IND`) is the key source for permit categories, requirements, renewals, decisions, and many official procedures.

Do not rely on community summaries alone when your status depends on the answer.

## Common routes

People may stay in the Netherlands through different routes, including:

- Work
- Study
- Family reunification or partner routes
- Highly skilled migrant routes
- Other specific categories

Each route has different rules for sponsorship, income, documents, work rights, and renewal timing.

## Renewals

If you already have a permit, do not wait until the last minute to review:

- Expiry date
- Required supporting documents
- Whether your situation has changed
- Whether you must act personally or through a sponsor

## Work, study, and family conditions

A permit may include conditions about:

- Whether you can work
- Whether the employer must meet special requirements
- Whether you can study
- Whether your permit depends on a partner, family member, or sponsor

Read the conditions attached to your own permit carefully.

## Community guide disclaimer

This is a community guide, not legal or immigration advice. For residence permits, always verify with official IND sources before making decisions or submitting documents.
